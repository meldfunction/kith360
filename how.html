How Your Circle Works

Same people. Many kinds of help. One simple system.

The Basics

Your circle = your 8-15 trusted people. Friends, neighbors, family, coworkers. Anyone you'd already ask for help.

Same people for everything. You don't build separate circles for childcare, rides, skills. It's the same 8-15 handling whatever comes up.

Fair tracking built-in. The app tracks who helps who. 1 hour = 1 credit, regardless of what kind of help. No mental tallies. No guilt.

You coordinate directly. No group chat chaos. When you need help, app notifies people who can help. You message 1:1 to work out details.
