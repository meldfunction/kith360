Pricing & Fees

You set your budget. Post what you're willing to pay. Providers apply if it works for them.

Platform fee: 5-15% added to what you pay (not taken from provider's rate).

Example: You hire a sitter at $20/hour for 4 hours. You pay $80 + $8 platform fee (10%) = $88 total. Sitter gets full $80.

Why a fee? Covers background checks, insurance verification, payment processing, customer support, and maintaining the vetted network.

Safety & Vetting

Multi-Layer Background Checks

State criminal records, federal databases, sex offender registry. Renewed annually. Any red flags = immediate removal.

Reference Verification

We call references. We verify past work. We check credentials. No automated rubber-stamping.

Insurance Requirements

All providers carry liability insurance. We verify coverage before approval. Protects you and them.

Ongoing Monitoring

Reviews from families. Complaint tracking. Performance monitoring. Bad actors get removed fast.

Secure Payments

Payment processed through Stripe (PCI-compliant). Money held in escrow until service confirmed. Dispute resolution built-in.

24/7 Support

Issues during service? Contact support anytime. We investigate immediately and take action.
